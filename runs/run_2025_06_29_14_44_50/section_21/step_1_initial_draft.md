## SECTION 21: Sellside Positioning - Competitive Positioning

Fu Yu Corporation Limited ("Fu Yu" or the "Company") is Singapore's oldest and one of Asia's largest manufacturers of high-end precision plastic and metal components and products. As of FY2024, the Group has strategically pivoted to focus solely on its core manufacturing business, having ceased all activities in its supply chain management services segment in the last quarter of FY2024. This analysis focuses on the competitive advantages of its continuing manufacturing operations.

While Fu Yu's manufacturing segment demonstrated a positive trajectory with revenue growth and margin improvement in FY2024, the Group as a whole remained in a net loss position (S\$3.9 million in FY2024, narrowed from S\$10.1 million in FY2023) due to the discontinued supply chain segment's losses and impairments. The ongoing investigation into Fu Yu Supply Chain Solutions Pte Ltd ("FYSCS") has resulted in a qualified audit opinion for FY2024, highlighting weaknesses in internal controls and potential financial claims, which presents a significant non-operational risk that could impact overall investor perception and financial flexibility, despite management's assertion that it does not impact continuing operations.
