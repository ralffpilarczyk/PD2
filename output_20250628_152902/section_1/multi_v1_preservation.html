This demonstrates a clear strategic investment in high-value, specialized manufacturing capabilities.
  • Vertical Integration: The Group's business model provides vertically-integrated manufacturing services, offering a "one-stop solution" for customers that includes product design, tooling design and fabrication, production, assembly, and testing [SOURCE: Fu Yu 24 AR_m.md, page 1].
