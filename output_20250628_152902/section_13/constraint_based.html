Corporate Strategies** * **Strategy 1: Elevating Precision Manufacturing with Smart Factory and Integrated Services** * **Description**: Fu Yu leverages its flagship Smart Factory in Singapore and comprehensive vertically-integrated services to deliver high-precision plastic and metal components and products, aiming to become a trusted manufacturing partner for innovative, high-performance products. This is achieved by offering end-to-end solutions from product design to assembly. * **Required Capabilities**: * **Advanced Smart Factory**: Flagship facility at 9 Tuas Drive 1 in Singapore, operational in FY2024, featuring 100K-class cleanroom, Industry 4.0 machinery, and capabilities for complex mould inserts, high-precision tooling, and 3D metal printing for rapid prototyping [Fu Yu 24 AR_m.md, p. 2, "COMPANY PROFILE"; p. 6, "CHAIRMAN'S MESSAGE"]. * **Vertically-Integrated Services**: Existing one-stop solution covering product design, tooling design and fabrication, production (plastic and metal injection moulding), assembly, and testing [Fu Yu 24 AR_m.md, p. 2, "COMPANY PROFILE"; p. 18, "2.1 Our Business"]. * **New Product Introduction (NPI) Team**: An established team capable of adding value in the early stages of the business cycle [Fu Yu 24 AR_m.md, p. 5, "CHAIRMAN'S MESSAGE"]. * **Competitive and Historical Context**: * **Historical Performance**: Core manufacturing segment showed a sharp improvement in FY2024, marking the first time in over a decade [Fu Yu 24 AR_m.md, p. 5, "CHAIRMAN'S MESSAGE"]. * **Market Traction**: Secured new projects in FY2024 across medical devices, drone technology, and smart appliances, including global leaders in wound care and autonomous drone systems [Fu Yu 24 AR_m.md, p. 6, "CHAIRMAN'S MESSAGE"]. * **Industry Positioning**: Affirms Fu Yu's position as one of Asia's most advanced precision manufacturing facilities [Fu Yu 24 AR_m.md, p. 6, "CHAIRMAN'S MESSAGE"]. * **Customer Relationships**: Long-term partnerships are fostered by providing a one-stop solution, enhancing value-add to customers [Fu Yu 24 AR_m.md, p. 2, "COMPANY PROFILE"]. * **Disconnects**: None identified.
